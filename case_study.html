The following product designs are of different major projects that I was a part of throughout my career, so far.

Edvoy Connect: Overview Dashboard

The Overview Dashboard was designed to give SRMs, team leads, and global heads a clear, real-time view of how their teams are performing. From weekly enquiries and contracts to student progress and regional trends, everything’s in one place — clean, organized, and easy to scan. With filters that adapt to each user’s role and charts that show what’s changing week over week, the dashboard helps teams stay on top of things and make faster, better decisions.

Edvoy Connect: Introducer Dashboard

The Introducer Dashboard was designed to make life easier for teams managing large networks of agents and introducers. Instead of digging through complex spreadsheets or scattered tools, users get a clear, real-time view of how introducers are performing—across stages like Enquired, Verified, Customer, and beyond. With simplified charts, filters, and lifecycle tracking, the dashboard helps users quickly spot what’s working, who’s active, and where support is needed. It’s a practical, no-fuss way to keep performance in check and teams aligned.

MGL - Demand Forecasting Tool

This product design highlights the creation of Mahanagar Gas Limited's (MGL) Demand Forecasting Platform, a comprehensive solution designed to optimize gas distribution operations. The project focused on developing a user-centric platform tailored to meet the specific needs of MGL and its stakeholders. Through meticulous design and implementation, the study delves into the process of crafting an end-to-end solution, from conceptualization to deployment. The platform's architecture, functionalities, and usability principles are examined, showcasing how it empowers MGL to accurately forecast demand, optimize resources, and ensure efficient gas distribution across the Mumbai metropolitan area.

Trackmile

Trackmile is a subsidiary of a Bangalore based logistic company called eShipz. The Trackmile mobile app is used by the delivery personal to update the shipping status of a Shipment. Once a delivery personal delivers a package, he will take a picture of the Proof Of Delivery (POD) and upload it to the app. And once he uploads the POD picture, he will also update the delivery status of the shipment in the app. Which will reflect in the client’s dash board at the warehouse.
